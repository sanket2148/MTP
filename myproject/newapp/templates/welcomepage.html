Revolutionizing Text-Image Annotation with Speech Input

How Talkscribe Works

Admins

Admins can upload images and assign them to annotators for annotation tasks. They can also annotate images themselves.

Annotators

Annotators receive assignments from admins and use speech input to annotate text-images accurately and efficiently for multiple languages.

Validators

Validators review annotations provided by annotators to ensure quality and accuracy.

Researchers

Researchers have access to annotated data and can download it for analysis and further research purposes.
